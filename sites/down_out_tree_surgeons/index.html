About Down & Out Tree Surgeons

Your trusted tree care experts serving Hastings and surrounding areas

At Down & Out Tree Surgeons, we understand that trees are more than just part of your landscape—they're valuable assets that provide beauty, shade, and environmental benefits. With years of experience in professional arboriculture, our team delivers comprehensive tree care solutions that prioritize both the health of your trees and the safety of your property.

Our certified arborists bring expertise, professionalism, and a genuine passion for tree care to every project. Whether you need routine maintenance, emergency tree removal, or expert consultation on tree health, we're here to help your landscape thrive.

We use industry-leading techniques and equipment to ensure every job is completed safely, efficiently, and to the highest standards. When you choose Down & Out Tree Surgeons, you're choosing peace of mind.
