About High Pro Tree Service

Orange Park's Most Trusted Tree Care Professionals

High Pro Tree Service LLC is your premier tree removal specialist serving Orange Park and the surrounding areas. Our team of certified arborists and skilled technicians brings expertise, professionalism, and state-of-the-art equipment to every job.
